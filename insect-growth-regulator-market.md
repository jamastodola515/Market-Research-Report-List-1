<p><strong>The Insect Growth Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chitin Synthesis Inhibitors</li><li>Juvenile Hormone Analogs & Mimics</li><li>Anti-juvenile Hormone Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Insect Growth Regulator (IGR) market encompasses three main types: Chitin Synthesis Inhibitors, which disrupt the formation of chitin, preventing proper development in insects; Juvenile Hormone Analogs & Mimics, which interfere with hormonal processes to mimic juvenile stages, hindering maturation; and Anti-juvenile Hormone Agents, which promote premature metamorphosis, leading to early death or malformation. These IGRs are vital for pest control, offering environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional insecticides while targeting specific developmental processes in insects.</p></p>
