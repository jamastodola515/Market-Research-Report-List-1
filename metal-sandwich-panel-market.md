<p><p>The metal sandwich panel market comprises several types, each serving distinct insulation needs. EPS sandwich panels use expanded polystyrene for lightweight and cost-effective insulation. PU sandwich panels employ polyurethane for superior thermal efficiency. Glass wool sandwich panels utilize fiberglass for excellent fire resistance and thermal insulation. PF sandwich panels, made of phenolic foam, offer enhanced fire performance and low thermal conductivity. Other sandwich panels include alternative materials or composites tailored for specific applications, expanding the market's versatility and appeal.</p></p>
