<p><p>An on-board power converter is a device used in vehicles to convert electrical power from one form to another, ensuring compatibility between different systems and components. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.4% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by stringent emission regulations, government incentives for EV adoption, and the expansion of charging infrastructure.</p><p>Key trends in the market include the development of compact, lightweight, and high-efficiency power converters, integration of advanced semiconductor materials like silicon carbide (SiC) and gallium nitride (GaN), and the rise of bidirectional power converters for vehicle-to-grid (V2G) applications. Additionally, the growing focus on autonomous vehicles and connected car technologies is further propelling the demand for advanced on-board power conversion systems. The Asia-Pacific region is expected to dominate the market due to rapid industrialization and the presence of major automotive manufacturers.</p></p>
