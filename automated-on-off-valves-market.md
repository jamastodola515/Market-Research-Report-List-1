<p><p>The Automated On-Off Valves market encompasses various types designed for controlling fluid flow. Angle valves are ideal for directing flow at an angle, offering compact installation. Ball valves provide quick shut-off with low resistance, suitable for high-pressure applications. Control valves adjust flow rates through precise modulation. Float valves automatically regulate water levels, commonly used in tanks. Other valve types include gate, check, and butterfly valves, each offering unique benefits for different industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
