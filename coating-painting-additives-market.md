<p><p>The Coating Additives Market includes various types that enhance paint performance. Dispersing agents improve pigment distribution, ensuring a uniform color. Anti-foam agents reduce foam during application, preventing defects. Rheology control agents adjust the flow and viscosity for better application. Leveling agents promote smooth surfaces by eliminating imperfections. Adhesion promoters enhance the bond between surfaces and coatings, improving durability. Other additives may include stabilizers, biocides, and UV absorbers, further optimizing paint properties and extending product lifespan.</p></p>
