<p><ul><li>Invasive Leak Detection</li><li>Non-Invasive Leak Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid leak sensors market is categorized into two main types: invasive and non-invasive leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves physically penetrating surfaces to access fluids, often providing precise location data but potentially causing damage during installation. Non-invasive leak detection, on the other hand, relies on external monitoring techniques, such as acoustic or thermal sensors, to identify leaks without needing to disrupt the surrounding environment. Both methods play vital roles in ensuring safety and efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
