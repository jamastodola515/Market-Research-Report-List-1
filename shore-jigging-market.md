<p><p>Shore jigging encompasses various jig types that differ in weight distribution. Front balanced jigs concentrate weight at the front, creating a rapid descent and effective fish targeting. Center balanced jigs offer a versatile design, allowing for a balanced swimming action, appealing to a wide range of fish species. Back balanced jigs, with their weight at the rear, provide a unique fluttering action, ideal for enticing predators. Each jig type serves specific fishing strategies and environments, catering to diverse angler preferences.</p></p>
