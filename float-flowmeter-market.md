<p><p>A Float Flowmeter is a device used to measure the flow rate of liquids and gases by utilizing a floating object, typically a ball or a cylindrical float, that moves up and down in a vertical tube as fluid flows through it. The position of the float correlates to the flow rate, and this measurement can be easily read from a scale. Float flowmeters are widely recognized for their simplicity, reliability, and low maintenance requirements, making them a popular choice in various industries such as water treatment, chemical processing, and oil and gas.</p><p>The Float Flowmeter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period.
