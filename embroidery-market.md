<p><p>The embroidery market encompasses various types, including surface embroidery, counted embroidery, and needlepoint. Surface embroidery involves stitching designs onto fabric without counting threads, allowing for more creativity and embellishment. Counted embroidery requires precise counting of fabric threads, enabling accurate placement of stitches, often resulting in intricate patterns. Needlepoint, a specific counted method, uses a canvas base and a tapestry needle to create textured images. Each type appeals to different skill levels and artistic preferences, contributing to the overall diversity of the market.</p></p>
