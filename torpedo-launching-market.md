<p><p>The torpedo launching market is primarily categorized into two types: underwater tubes and deck-mounted units. In contrast, deck-mounted units are positioned on surface vessels, enabling quick deployment and ease of access for launching torpedoes. Both types cater to distinct operational requirements, allowing naval forces to effectively engage underwater threats based on their strategic needs.</p></p>
