<p><p>Passive Infrared Detectors (PIR) are sensors that detect infrared radiation emitted by objects, particularly humans. In the market, there are two primary types: single-beam and multi-beam systems. Single-beam PIRs cover a specific area with one detection zone, ideal for smaller spaces. In contrast, multi-beam PIRs utilize multiple detection zones, providing broader coverage and enhanced sensitivity, making them suitable for larger or complex environments. These variations cater to different security and automation needs across residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
