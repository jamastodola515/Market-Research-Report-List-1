<p><p>The prebiotic fiber market includes various types that promote gut health. Inulin, derived from plants like chicory, is known for its soluble fiber benefits. Mannan-Oligosaccharide (MOS), often sourced from yeast, supports digestive health and immunity. Fructo-Oligosaccharide (FOS) is a sweet-tasting prebiotic found in many fruits and vegetables, enhancing gut flora. Galacto-Oligosaccharide (GOS), primarily derived from lactose, is effective in stimulating beneficial bacteria. These prebiotics play a vital role in nutrition and wellness, driving market growth.</p></p>
