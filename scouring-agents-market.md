<p><ul><li>Anionic Scouring Agent</li><li>Cationic Scouring Agent</li><li>Nonionic Scouring Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scouring agents market comprises three primary types: anionic, cationic, and nonionic agents. Anionic scouring agents are negatively charged and effective in removing dirt and grease, making them suitable for various cleaning applications. Cationic scouring agents, positively charged, often provide enhanced antibacterial properties but may be less effective in alkaline conditions. Nonionic scouring agents are neutral and versatile, ideal for delicate fabrics and materials, offering mild cleaning without excessive foaming, making them popular in both industrial and household applications.</p></p>
