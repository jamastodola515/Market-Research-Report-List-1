<p><p>Charge-coupled devices (CCDs) are sensors that convert light into electronic signals, widely used in imaging applications. The market features various types: Line CCDs capture images line-by-line, ideal for high-speed scanning; Interline CCDs enable simultaneous exposure and readout, minimizing motion blur; Full-Frame CCDs capture an entire frame at once, offering high image quality but slower readout; and Frame-Transfer CCDs quickly move captured frames to a storage area for faster processing. These variations cater to specific imaging needs across industries.</p></p>
