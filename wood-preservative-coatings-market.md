<p><p>The wood preservative coatings market includes water-based, solvent-based, and other types. Water-based coatings use water as a solvent, offering eco-friendly, low-VOC solutions with easy cleanup and minimal odor. Solvent-based coatings use organic solvents, providing durable, weather-resistant finishes but with higher VOC emissions. Other types include oil-based or hybrid formulations, balancing performance and environmental impact. Each type caters to specific needs, such as indoor/outdoor use, durability, or sustainability, influencing their adoption in construction, furniture, and industrial applications.</p></p>
