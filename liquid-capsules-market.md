<p><p>The liquid capsules market is primarily divided into two main types: gelatin and vegetarian capsules. Gelatin capsules are derived from animal sources, typically collagen, and are known for their easy digestibility and ability to encapsulate a wide range of liquids. Conversely, vegetarian capsules are made from plant-based materials, such as hydroxypropyl methylcellulose (HPMC), catering to the growing demand for vegan and vegetarian-friendly options. Both types are used extensively in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries for their convenience and efficacy.</p></p>
