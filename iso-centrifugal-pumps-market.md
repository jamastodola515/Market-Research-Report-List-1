<p><p>The ISO centrifugal pumps market is categorized into horizontal and vertical types, each designed for specific applications. Horizontal ISO centrifugal pumps are commonly used for transporting fluids at a horizontal orientation, offering efficient flow rates and ease of maintenance. In contrast, vertical ISO centrifugal pumps are designed for compact spaces and deep well applications, utilizing a vertical orientation to draw fluids from below. Both types adhere to ISO standards, ensuring reliability and performance in various industrial settings.</p></p>
