<p><strong>The Cold Rolled Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Pipe</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Cold Rolled Plate** market serves industries requiring high-strength, precision-finished steel. **Spiral Weld Pipe** uses cold-rolled plates coiled and welded spirally, ideal for pipelines and structural applications. **Electric Resistance Weld (ERW)** pipes are formed by rolling plates and welding edges using electric resistance, commonly used in construction and automotive sectors. **Double Submerged Arc Weld (DSAW)** pipes involve welding plates submerged under flux, offering superior strength for heavy-duty applications like oil and gas transportation. Each method leverages cold-rolled plates for durability and precision.</p></p>
