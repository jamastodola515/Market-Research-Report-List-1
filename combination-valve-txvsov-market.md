<p><p>The Combination Valve (TXV + SOV) market includes Normal Open (NO) and Normal Closed (NC) types. In NO valves, the valve remains open when de-energized, allowing refrigerant flow, and closes when energized, ideal for systems requiring continuous cooling. NC valves remain closed when de-energized, blocking refrigerant flow, and open when energized, suitable for systems needing controlled or intermittent cooling. Both types integrate Thermostatic Expansion Valves (TXV) and Solenoid Valves (SOV) to regulate refrigerant flow efficiently, catering to diverse HVAC and refrigeration applications.</p></p>
