<p><p>The wireless handheld spectrometer market is divided into several types, including Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR), Near-Infrared (NIR), and Raman spectrometers, among others. FTIR spectrometers are used for identifying chemical compounds by analyzing molecular vibrations. NIR spectrometers focus on materials' absorption in the near-infrared region, ideal for food and pharmaceutical industries. Raman spectrometers utilize inelastic scattering of light to provide molecular information, widely used in material analysis. Other types may include ultraviolet-visible (UV-Vis) and fluorescence spectrometers, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
