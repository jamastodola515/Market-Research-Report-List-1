<p><p>The silicone hydrogel contact lens market is characterized by three primary types: spherical, toric, and multifocal forms. Spherical lenses correct basic refractive errors like myopia and hyperopia. Toric lenses are designed for astigmatism, offering precise vision correction by compensating for varying curvature in the eye. Multifocal lenses address presbyopia, providing multiple focal points to facilitate clear vision at various distances. This segmentation caters to diverse visual needs, promoting comfort and health due to the breathable nature of silicone hydrogel materials.</p></p>
