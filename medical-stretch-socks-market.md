<p><p>The medical stretch socks market primarily consists of gradient compression socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient compression socks apply varying pressure, highest at the ankle and gradually decreasing up the leg, promoting blood circulation and reducing edema. They are commonly used by athletes and individuals with venous disorders. Anti-embolism socks, on the other hand, offer consistent compression to prevent deep vein thrombosis in patients who are immobile, especially during surgeries or long recovery periods. Together, they address different medical and lifestyle needs.</p></p>
