<p><p>The frozen bakery market encompasses several types, primarily focusing on convenience and quality. Breads include pre-baked options that require baking before consumption, maintaining freshness. Ready-to-thaw products are fully baked and designed to be thawed and served without further cooking, offering immediate use. Ready-to-prove items, on the other hand, are part-baked dough that requires proofing and baking, allowing for a fresh-out-of-the-oven experience while simplifying the baking process for both consumers and foodservice operations.</p></p>
