<p><strong>The Electric Compressor Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Row Angular Contact Ball Bearing</li><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric compressor bearing market features primarily two types: double row angular contact ball bearings and deep groove ball bearings. Double row angular contact ball bearings are designed to handle both radial and axial loads, making them ideal for high-speed applications in electric compressors. In contrast, deep groove ball bearings are versatile and can accommodate radial loads while providing low friction and high reliability, making them suitable for various compressor designs. Both types enhance efficiency and performance in electric compressor systems.</p></p>
