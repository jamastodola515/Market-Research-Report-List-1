<p><p>The Automotive Rear View Camera Lens market primarily features two types: CCD and CMOS cameras. CCD cameras offer high image quality, better low-light performance, and reduced noise, making them ideal for premium vehicles. CMOS cameras, on the other hand, are cost-effective, energy-efficient, and compact, making them popular in mass-market vehicles. Both technologies enhance safety by providing clear rear-view visibility, but CCD cameras are preferred for superior image clarity, while CMOS cameras dominate due to affordability and widespread adoption in mid-range and entry-level cars.</p></p>
