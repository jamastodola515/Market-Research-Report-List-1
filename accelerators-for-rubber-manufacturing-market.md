<p><p>Accelerators in rubber manufacturing enhance the curing process, improving efficiency and product properties. Common types include MBT (Mercaptobenzothiazole), MBTS (Dibenzothiazole disulfide), CBS (N-Cyclohexyl-2-benzothiazole sulfenamide), TBBS (N-Tertiarybutyl-2-benzothiazole sulfenamide), and other specialty accelerators. MBT and MBTS facilitate faster vulcanization, while CBS and TBBS provide improved heat resistance and processing characteristics. These accelerators play a crucial role in determining the performance, durability, and elasticity of rubber products, catering to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
